<h2 id="about-me" class="home-section-title">👋 About Me</h2>

Welcome to my homepage. My work sits at the intersection of `Computational Pathology` and `AI for Bioinformatics`. My published research spans whole-slide image analysis, biomarker prediction, and drug-target interaction modeling. My current unpublished work mainly focuses on whole-slide pathology image analysis, involving Mamba-based modeling, agent-style reasoning, and related theoretical derivation. Outside research, I enjoy fitness with my good buddies [Chao](https://weipengchao.top/) and [Le](https://dutianle.ltd/), and Teamfight Tactics（金铲铲）.
